Blanchardstown Local History

Institite of Technology Blanchardstown is located in Blanchardstown, the college brings a lot to the area, but the location which the college is in has a big influence to the sucess of the college. Here I am just going to run you through a few facts about Blanchardstown local history.

History Facts about Blanchardstown

  • The name Blanchardstown comes from the Blanchard family, who were granted their estate some time between 1250 and 1260.
  • Blanchardstown was a predominantly rural area, with a small village
  • It is located in western County Dublin, alongside the neighbouring district of Castleknock.
  • Blanchardstown has a great choice for transport.
  • There are three Irish Rail train stations in the wider Blanchardstown area: Castleknock, Coolmine and Clonsilla.
  • Dublin bus and Go-Ahead Ireland routes pass through Blanchardstown, Bus Eireann and Express bus routes are also an option from Blanchardstown.
  • Connolly Hospital, one of Dublin's main hospitals, and a public university teaching hospital, is located in the area.
  • Blanchardstown Centre has a large retail shopping centre with over 180 stores, there is also a retail park located beside the Blanchardstown Centre.
  • There are seven Primary Schools located in the area.
  • There are four Secondary Schools in Blanchardstown
  • And there is one Third Level Education College loctaed here.
  • The National Sports Campus is located in Blanchardstown, and this includes the National Aquatic Centre
  • There are twelve local chruches located around Blanchardstown.
