History of ITB

We are going to take a little trip back in time to see exactly where ITB came from and go through some of its milestones!

Timeline of ITB History

/

1999

This is when ITB first opened its doors, with the only building being A Block, which still remains today.

1999-2006

Here we have a poster that was made of the opening of the new college in Blanchardstown, on it we can the logo that was fisrt used for ITB, ITB used this logo from when they opened in 1999 to 2006 when they felt they needed a more simplistic colour palate.

2003

This was a big year for ITB as the institute issed its first Degrees!

2004

This was another big year for ITB, as they had their offical grand openning.
They also started a big build project this year, as they started really thinking about the future of ITB.
ITB also joined the social media game, as they made a Bebo page, a place for students and staff to interact!

2006

ITB underwent a chang of look this year as they chnaged their logo, from the one shown earlier, to a new one with more simple colours.

2008

In September 2008 ITB launched its Bachelor of Arts (honours) in Creative Digital Media. This was a course where students were allowed to be creative and experimental. Graduates of the BA in Creative Digital Media will have the knowledge and skills to take up employment in a broad number of areas within the digital media industry.

2009

ITB 10 Year Celebration!
ITB celebrated this great year with many differnt events, such as;

  • Community days
  • Staff reunion
  • 10 year logo

2012

2012 was the year were there were more thoughts going into the future of ITB and there was talks about joing with ITT And DIT to create a Technological University.

2016

The legislation for Technological University Dublin was debated at this time, however the governmemt fell through.

2018

The big year for change!
In March 2018 the legislation for Technological University Dublin was passed and signed into law.
On the 17th of July was the announcement of TU Dublin.
TU Dublin will be set in place from January 2019!
